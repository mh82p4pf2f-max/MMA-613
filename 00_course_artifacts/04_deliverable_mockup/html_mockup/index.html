Labour Market Stress & Social Support Prioritization Dashboard

Governed decision-support for policy review & triage · MMA 616 · design mockup of the nine intended views

⚠️ Triage signal only. This dashboard flags areas for human policy review. It does not determine social-assistance need, eligibility, or benefits. Labour-market rates derive from the real LFS; income, low-income, housing & population shown here are 🧪 synthetic demonstration data (clearly flagged) so every view is fully populated. The live Streamlit app also offers the real governed panel (Knowledge/processed/policy_triage_panel.csv), where genuinely missing inputs lower confidence and are never invented.
This is a static design mockup (no server, no live data). The working dashboard is the Streamlit app at dashboard/app.py. See ../mockup_notes.md. Numbers shown are illustrative only.
