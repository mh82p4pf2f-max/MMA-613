# knowledge/ — the project's Knowledge stock

This folder is the **Knowledge stock** from the Agentic Project Design loop: the curated source material Claude reads and reasons over. Per the course method, its quality ceiling is set by what we choose to put here — so the discipline is **curation, not accumulation**: connect the primary material, keep the binding facts tight and current, and augment with the domain understanding the raw data cannot supply.

## Contents

- **[managing-intelligence/managing-intelligence.md](managing-intelligence/managing-intelligence.md)** — The full course textbook, *Managing Intelligence: Strategic Leadership in the Age of AI* (Glaser & Kotadia, 2026), in Markdown. The method (Plan → Build → Use → Evaluate), the six principles, the five capability forms, the five-layer evaluation model, and the worked Kinquiry example. Read this to keep our work aligned with how the course expects the loop to be run.
- **[assignment/assignment-brief.md](assignment/assignment-brief.md)** — The official instructor brief (faithful capture) plus a requirements compliance checklist mapping every graded component to our project, and the key dates for this cohort.
